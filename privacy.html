Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

Overview

Grateful Together ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Gratitude Messages: The daily appreciation messages you write for your partner.
  • Partner Connection: Information about your paired partner relationship within the app.
  • App Usage: Which apps you choose to block (stored locally on your device via Apple's Screen Time API).

How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate the exchange of gratitude messages between you and your partner.
  • To track your streak and progress.
  • To send push notifications about your partner's activity.
  • To improve our app and user experience.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, a Google Cloud service with industry-standard security measures. Your gratitude messages are encrypted in transit and at rest.

App blocking preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's Screen Time framework and are never transmitted to our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your gratitude messages are only shared with your paired partner within the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your gratitude history
  • Unpair from your partner at any time
